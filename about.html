About

So, who am I?

I'm Eric Boyle, a 21-year-old student originally from Oshawa, Ontario. My educational journey has been diverse, encompassing various fields of study. I hold a one-year Business certificate from Durham College, and currently, I'm studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. This path allows me to fuse my creativity with technological know-how, shaping a unique approach to my academic pursuits.

Exploring interactive media design has been a revelation for me. It's not just about pixels and screens; it's a gateway to capturing the beauty of everyday moments through photography. Additionally, I prioritize my physical well-being, finding solace and focus in regular workouts. The discipline cultivated in the gym resonates throughout my academic and creative endeavors, creating a harmonious balance in my life.

As I navigate through my academic journey, I'm constantly refining my skills in interactive media design. I thrive on the opportunity to contribute fresh and innovative ideas to the dynamic digital landscape. I invite you to join me on this adventure of exploration and creativity, where every pixel serves a purpose, and design shapes the future. Together, let's explore the endless possibilities where creativity meets technology.
