Meet the Founder

Bio

Eric Boyle, is a 21-year-old student originally from Oshawa, Ontario, has a diverse educational background, which includes a one-year Business certificate from Durham College. He is currently immersing himself in the world of Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College and brings a unique blend of creativity and technological know-how to his academic pursuits!

Being in interactive media design has allowed Eric to explore photography and provides him with the means to freeze-frame the beauty of everyday moments. Eric also prioritizes his physical well-being through regular workouts. The discipline and focus cultivated in the gym spill over into his academic and creative pursuits, creating a harmonious balance in his life.

As he navigates his academic journey, Eric Boyle is constantly refining his skills in interactive media design. He is always eager to contribute fresh and innovative ideas to the ever-evolving digital landscape, he invites you to join him on this adventure of exploration and creativity, where pixels meet purpose, and design meets the future.
